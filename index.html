Hi! This is Irine

I am currently pursuing a Master of Management in Auckland, New Zealand while working part-time and balancing family life.

More About Me

I am expected to complete my Master of Management and pursue a career where I can apply the management knowledge gained from my degree, together with the skills I have developed over time, to support organisational operations. I aim for roles that involve administrative coordination, data management, and the use of digital tools. I am also open to the possibility of pursuing entrepreneurial opportunities in the future, as it requires management and operational skills.

My Strength

One of my strengths is being responsible and disciplined in fulfilling my responsibilities. I try to clearly understand my work scope and always aim to complete my tasks to the best of my ability while managing my time effectively and maintaining punctuality. I am also comfortable learning independently when the learning objectives and resources are clear. In addition, through my previous experience in accounting support and inventory administration, I developed an interest in working with data and records. I enjoy organising information and analysing data to understand what information is needed and how it can be used effectively.

My Areas of Development

I recognise several areas where I can continue to improve. One area is communication, particularly when speaking in front of groups or responding without preparation. I am more comfortable communicating when I clearly understand the context of a discussion. I would also like to improve my ability to communicate ideas and clarify situations within a team environment, especially when decisions involve multiple people. In addition, I aim to continue developing my leadership and teamwork skills by gaining more experience working with different people and organisational environments.
